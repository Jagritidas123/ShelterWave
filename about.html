About Us

ShelterWave is dedicated to providing assistance to refugees in need. Our mission is to connect individuals and communities with various NGOs and organizations that support displaced persons, ensuring that no one is left behind.

What We Do

We compile and curate links to various reputable NGOs that offer assistance to refugees, ensuring that users can find the help they need quickly and efficiently. Our platform aims to simplify the donation process and increase support for organizations working tirelessly to aid those in crisis.

Our Vision

We envision a world where all refugees receive the support they need to thrive, regardless of their circumstances. Through collaboration and community engagement, we strive to create lasting change and uplift those who have been displaced

Get Involved

Join us in making a difference! Whether your are looking to donate, volunteer, or simply spread awareness about the refugee crisis, every action counts. Together, we can create a wave of support for those who need it most.
